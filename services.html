Send us your list of plants for a job or for inventory and we will send you the fob point, the size of the plant, and a quote or estiamted "landed price". We pride ourselves on our ability to quickly find plants from our extensive vendor list and will source plants quickly while you spend your time running your business.

freight shipping

GGS has close relationships with Northland Express Transport, John Beach & Sons Trucking, and Penske Truck Rentals. We can arrange freight for nursery stock from all of our vendors, whether they are in Oregon, Ohio, Florida, North Carolina, or Michigan. Our truck brokers specialize in hauling nursery stock - and we will lock in a rate before your order is loaded. In special circumstances we will rent a truck, and bring it ourselves. We understand the importance of freight, and have the experience to offer you the best service avialable.
