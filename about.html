about kris

Kris comes form a long lineage of horticulturalists. Both his grandfather and father owned nurseries and greenhouses accross the Midwest. Kris himself has over 30 years experience in the horticultural industry, both as a nusery owner and long-time wholesale distributor.

Great Green Sources is his effort to build a full service distributor focused on cultivating lasting relationships and delivering plants products from long-trusted vendors.

Kris lives is Ada, Michigan with his wife Betsy and two dogs Freida and Zelda. He graduated from Michigan State University in 1977 with a degree in Ornamental Horticulture.
